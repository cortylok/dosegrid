## Uganda (UG)

**Regulatory note:** The National Drug Authority (NDA) registers medicines and maintains the National Drug Register; products carry an NDA reg number. Class A = prescription-only; Class B/C = pharmacy/general sale. Most simple analgesics, antihistamines and antacids are sold OTC through pharmacies and licensed drug shops. Kampala Pharmaceutical Industries (KPI) is the largest local manufacturer (60+ generics). [NDA National Drug Register July 2024](https://www.nda.or.ug/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/NATIONAL-DRUG-REGISTER-OF-UGANDA-HUMAN-MEDICINES-JULY-2024-1.pdf) · [KPI products](https://www.kpi.co.ug/)

## Ethiopia (ET)

**Regulatory note:** The Ethiopian Food and Drug Authority (EFDA) classifies medicines as OTC vs prescription-only (POM) and publishes a national OTC Medicines List (3rd edition, July 2022, 128 medicines, listed by INN/generic). The market is largely generic/INN-named — local manufacturers (EPHARM, Addis Pharmaceuticals Factory/APF, Cadila Ethiopia, Julphar) and Indian imports dominate; consumer "brand" recognition is lower than in West/East Africa. Strengths/forms below are taken from the EFDA OTC list (authoritative). [EFDA OTC Medicines List, 3rd ed.](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf) · [EPHARM](https://epharm.et/)

> Note on OTC strength cut-offs (EFDA rule): aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid) is OTC only at 300 mg and above — lower (cardio) strengths are POM. Salbutamol aerosol/syrup OTC but tablet POM.

### Analgesics / Antipyretics / NSAIDs
- **Paracetamol** → EPHARM Paracetamol (500 mg tab; also 100 mg/5 mL drops, 120/250 mg/5 mL syrup; 100/500 mg tab; 125/250 mg suppository), APF/Cadila paracetamol generics, Panadol (imported). OTC; max single dispense 20 tabs, max daily 4 g. [EFDA OTC list §3.3](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf) · [EPHARM products](https://epharm.et/)
- **Paracetamol + caffeine** → generic combos (500 mg + 30/50/65 mg tab). OTC. [EFDA OTC list §3.3](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf)
- **Ibuprofen** → ibuprofen generics (200, 300, 400 mg tab/cap; 100 mg/5 mL susp; 75/150 mg suppository; 5%/10% gel). OTC; max 20 tabs. [EFDA OTC list §3.3](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf)
- **Aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid)** → ASA generics (300, 500 mg tab). OTC at ≥300 mg. [EFDA OTC list §3.3](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf)
- **Diclofenac** → diclofenac diethylamine topical gel (1%, 1.16%, 2.32%; combos with methyl salicylate/menthol). Topical OTC; oral diclofenac POM. [EFDA OTC list §3.3/§3.5](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf)
- **Indomethacin** → indomethacin 100 mg suppository (OTC per EFDA musculoskeletal list). [EFDA OTC list §3.5](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf)

### Antihistamines
- **Cetirizine** → cetirizine generics (10 mg tab) — imported brands incl. Zyrtec. OTC/pharmacy. [EFDA OTC list §3.9 Antihistamines](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf) (UNVERIFIED specific local brand)
- **Chlorphenamine (chlorpheniramine)** → chlorpheniramine maleate (4 mg; in combo cough syrups). OTC. [EFDA OTC list §3.2/§3.3](https://www.efda.gov.et/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/OTC-medicines-list.pdf)
- **Loratadine** → loratadine generics (10 mg tab). OTC/pharmacy. (UNVERIFIED local brand/strength on EFDA list)

### Antacids / GI / PPIs / H2

## Tunisia (TN)
