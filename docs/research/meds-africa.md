## South Africa (ZA)

**Regulatory note:** SAHPRA schedules medicines S0 (general sale — any retailer), S1 (pharmacist-initiated, may be advertised), S2 (pharmacist-authorised, no Rx), S3+ (prescription). Most OTC analgesics are S0–S2; codeine combinations are S2 (small packs, pharmacist-controlled). Single-ingredient oral pseudoephedrine is no longer sold OTC; combination cold products remain available. [SAHPRA consolidated schedules](https://www.sahpra.org.za/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Consolidated-Schedules_01-August-2025.pdf) · [SAHPRA OTC directory](https://medsinfo.sahpra.org.za/)

### Analgesics / Antipyretics / NSAIDs
- **Paracetamol** → Panado (500 mg tab, 120 mg/5 mL syrup, paediatric drops, effervescent), Calpol (paediatric susp), Compral (paracetamol + caffeine + meprobamate — headache powder/tab), Panamol. OTC (S0/S1). [Dis-Chem Panado](https://www.dischem.co.za/panado-tablets-24-s-859) · [Dis-Chem analgesics](https://www.dischem.co.za/shop-by-department/health/self-medication/analgesics/pain-and-fever)
- **Ibuprofen** → Brufen (200 mg, 400 mg, 600 mg tab; 100 mg/5 mL susp), Nurofen (200 mg, 400 mg), Ibugesic, Mypaid. OTC low-strength S1/S2. [Dis-Chem analgesics](https://www.dischem.co.za/shop-by-department/health/self-medication/analgesics/pain-and-fever)
- **Aspirin** → Disprin (300 mg dispersible), Ecotrin (81 mg, 300 mg enteric — low-dose cardio), Aspirin generics. OTC. [Dis-Chem analgesics](https://www.dischem.co.za/shop-by-department/health/self-medication/analgesics/pain-and-fever)
- **Diclofenac** → Voltaren (topical Emulgel 1%; oral tabs Rx), Catafast, Cataflam (50 mg sachet/tab). Oral generally S3 Rx; topical OTC. [Dis-Chem](https://www.dischem.co.za/shop-by-department/health/self-medication/analgesics/pain-and-fever)

- **Paracetamol/ibuprofen + codeine combos** → Myprodol (codeine phosphate 10 mg + ibuprofen 200 mg + paracetamol 250 mg, cap/tab), Mybulen (similar), Gen-Payne (codeine + ibuprofen + paracetamol cap). ## Nigeria (NG)

**Regulatory note:** NAFDAC registers and regulates medicines; brands carry a NAFDAC reg number. Many analgesics/antipyretics are sold OTC through pharmacies and patent medicine vendors. Codeine-containing cough syrups were banned/restricted by NAFDAC in 2018; remaining OTC cough products use dextromethorphan/diphenhydramine. [NAFDAC Greenbook](https://greenbook.nafdac.gov.ng/)
