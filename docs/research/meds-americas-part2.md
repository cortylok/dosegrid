## Guatemala (GT)

### Analgesics / Antipyretics / NSAIDs
- **Paracetamol (acetaminofén)** → Tempra (Bayer; 500 mg tab; 100 mg/mL infant drops; 160 mg/5 mL jarabe), Panadol (GSK/Haleon; 500 mg), Tylenol (Johnson & Johnson; 500 mg tab; pediatric jarabe), MK Paracetamol (750 mg, 500 mg tab — Tecnoquímicas generic). OTC. [paiz.com.gt, walmart.com.gt, drugs.com/international]
- **Ibuprofeno** → Advil (Pfizer; 200/400 mg), Motrin, Select Pharma Ibuprofeno (400 mg coated tab; 100 mg/5 mL jarabe), Dolofin (regional Central America brand; 400 mg). OTC ≤400 mg. [selectpharma.com.gt]
- **Naproxeno sódico** → Apronax (Bayer; 275/550 mg — common Central America brand), Naproliv (Select Pharma; 550 mg), Flanax (regional). OTC at lower strengths. [selectpharma.com.gt, drugs.com/international]
- **Ácido acetilsalicílico (aspirina)** → Aspirina/Aspirina Bayer (100 mg low-dose, 500 mg), Mejoral (500 mg, with/without caffeine — regional brand). OTC. [drugs.com/international] (UNVERIFIED strengths)
- **Diclofenaco** → Voltaren (Novartis/GSK; 50 mg tab; 1% gel topical), Cataflam (diclofenac potassium 50 mg), Select Pharma Diclofenaco (sódico/potásico). Oral generally Rx; topical gel OTC. [selectpharma.com.gt]
- **Metamizol sódico (dipirona)** → Conmel (550 mg tab), Neo-Melubrina (500 mg tab; 500 mg/mL drops), Dolo-Neurobión combos. Widely available OTC in Guatemala/Central America. [tuasaude.com, selectpharma.com.gt]

### Antihistamines
- **Loratadina** → Clarityne / Claritin (Bayer; 10 mg tab; 5 mg/5 mL jarabe), Select Pharma Loratadina, MK Loratadina (generic). OTC. [selectpharma.com.gt, drugs.com/international]
- **Desloratadina** → Aviant / Aerius (5 mg tab; 0.5 mg/mL jarabe), Select Pharma Desloratadina. OTC. [selectpharma.com.gt]
- **Cetirizina** → Zyrtec, Reactine, generics (10 mg tab; 5 mg/5 mL). OTC. [drugs.com/international] (UNVERIFIED local brand)
- **Clorfenamina (clorfeniramina maleato)** → generic 4 mg tab; component of many antigripales. OTC. [selectpharma.com.gt]

### Antacids / GI / PPIs / H2
- **Antiácidos** → Sal Andrews (effervescent antacid — sodium bicarbonate + citric/tartaric acid), Alka-Seltzer (ASA + bicarbonate), Melox / Mylanta (aluminum + magnesium hydroxide + simethicone), Pepto-Bismol (bismuth subsalicylate; 4 oz / 118 mL suspension, 262 mg chewable tab). OTC. [cruzverde.com.gt]
- **Omeprazol** → Omeprazol generics (20 mg cap), various brands. OTC/Rx borderline. [drugs.com/international] (UNVERIFIED local brand)
- **Loperamida** → Imodium (2 mg), generics. OTC. [en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antimotility_agent]
- **Hioscina (butilescopolamina, butilhioscina)** → Buscapina (Sanofi/Boehringer; 10 mg coated tab), Buscapina Compositum (hioscina 10 mg + metamizol). Tab OTC. [drugs.com/otc Buscapina, parafarmacia-online.com]

### Decongestants / Cough / Cold
- **Antigripales combinados** → Tabcin (Bayer; day/night cold combos — paracetamol + decongestant + antihistamine), Tabcin Noche, Desenfriol-D (cold tablets; pseudoephedrine combo), Desenfriol-ito Plus (children's chewable), Mejoralito (children's). OTC. [farmaciasguadalajara.com, drugs.com/international]
- **Pseudoefedrina / fenilefrina** → component of Sudafed and the antigripal combos above. OTC (pseudoephedrine may be controlled at retail). [en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pseudoephedrine]
- **Ambroxol** → Mucosolvan (Boehringer Ingelheim; 30 mg tab; 30 mg/5 mL & 15 mg/5 mL jarabe; 7.5 mg/mL drops), generics. OTC. [drugs.com/international/ambroxol.html]
- **Dextrometorfano / guaifenesina** → components of Robitussin and combo cough syrups. OTC. (UNVERIFIED local brand)

### Sleep / Antiemetic / Motion
- **Dimenhidrinato** → Dramamine (50 mg tab; infant jarabe 120 mL, for ages 2–6), generics. OTC. [farmaciasguadalajara.com, en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dimenhydrinate]
- **Meclizina (meclizina)** → generics for motion sickness/vertigo (25 mg). OTC. [en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dimenhydrinate] (UNVERIFIED local brand)
- **Melatonina** → sold as supplement (1/3/5 mg) in pharmacies. OTC. (UNVERIFIED local brand)

## Panama (PA)

*(Brand list drawn from the AAC Panamá 2024 OTC reference guide — confirms specific brands marketed in Panama. CALOX supplies local house brands. Major chain: Farmacias Arrocha.)*

### Analgesics / Antipyretics / NSAIDs
- **Acetaminofén (paracetamol)** → Tylenol (500 mg tab; jarabe), Panadol (500 mg; Panadol Ultra migraña = acetaminofén + cafeína; Panadol gripe día / multisíntomas combos), Tempra (pediatric). OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Ibuprofeno** → Advil (200/400 mg), Dorival (Panamanian ibuprofeno brand), Motrin. OTC ≤400 mg. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Naproxeno** → Apronax (Bayer; 275/550 mg), naproxeno generics. OTC lower strengths. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Aspirina (ácido acetilsalicílico)** → Aspirina/Aspirina Bayer (100 mg, 500 mg), Cafiaspirina. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Ketorolaco / dexketoprofeno** → listed as OTC analgesics in the AAC guide (short-course). [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Topical pain** → Zepol, Cofal (ungüento muscular), parche de lidocaína, capsaicina. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Dipirona (metamizol)** → Conmel, Novalgina — available in Panama/Central America (Rx/OTC borderline). [drugs.com/international] (UNVERIFIED local brand)

### Antihistamines
- **Loratadina** → Clarityne / Claritin (10 mg tab; jarabe), loratadina generics. Non-sedating. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Fexofenadina** → Allegra (60/120/180 mg). Non-sedating. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Cetirizina** → Zyrtec, cetirizina generics (10 mg tab; 5 mg/5 mL). Sedating. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Levocetirizina** → Xyzal, generics (5 mg). OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Clorfenamina (clorfeniramina maleato)** → generic 2–4 mg tab; component of antigripales. Sedating. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Histagrip (CALOX)** → antihistamine/cold combo (jarabe 120 mL). OTC. [caloxca.com]

### Antacids / GI / PPIs / H2
- **Antiácidos** → Maalox / Gastrigel (hidróxido de aluminio), Tums (carbonato de calcio), leche de magnesia / Acigel (hidróxido de magnesio), Frutacid (CALOX; chewable, fruit flavor x60). OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide, caloxca.com]
- **PPIs** → Esomeprazol, lansoprazol, omeprazol, pantoprazol, rabeprazol — listed as OTC PPIs (20 mg). [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **H2 blockers** → cimetidina, famotidina, nizatidina (20/40 mg). OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Bismuto** → Pepto-Bismol (subsalicilato de bismuto). OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Loperamida** → Imodium (2 mg) — note guide flags caution in acute illness. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Hioscina (butilescopolamina)** → Buscapina (10 mg tab). OTC. [drugs.com/international] (UNVERIFIED local brand)

### Decongestants / Cough / Cold
- **Descongestionantes nasales** → oximetazolina (Afrin, Otrivina, Respibien — spray), fenilefrina (Loramed D, Claryten/Clarityne D, Dimegan D, Sensibit D, Panadol gripe día / multisíntomas), pseudoefedrina. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Guaifenesina** → expectorant component of cough/cold products. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Dextrometorfano** → cough suppressant ("DM" products); guide flags PM/DM combos. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Ambroxol** → Mucosolvan (30 mg tab; jarabe), Bropix (CALOX; cough syrup 35 mg/5 mL x 100 mL), ambroxol generics. OTC. [caloxca.com, drugs.com/international/ambroxol.html]

### Sleep / Antiemetic / Motion
- **Dimenhidrinato** → Dramamine, Gravol (50 mg tab; Gravidex injection from CALOX), generics. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide, caloxca.com]
- **Viajesan (CALOX)** → dimenhidrinato 50 mg x50 tab (motion sickness). OTC. [caloxca.com]
- **Doxilamina** → sleep aid (often + B6); guide flags sedation. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Difenhidramina** → sleep aid / PM products (e.g. Tylenol PM, Advil PM); sedating. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]
- **Melatonina** → supplement (1/3/5 mg) — guide notes it is not an antihistamine. OTC. [AAC Panamá 2024 OTC guide]

**Notes & caveats:**
- Metamizol/dipyrone (Conmel, Novalgina, Neo-Melubrina) is OTC and widely used across all five markets — unlike US/Canada where it is banned.
- Many lines tagged `(UNVERIFIED)` reflect that the specific local *brand* or *strength* could not be confirmed from a primary source, though the generic is marketed in-country; the generic + class is reliable.
- OTC vs Rx status for omeprazole and some NSAIDs is borderline / pharmacy-dependent in these markets.
- Spanish strength/form terms used: comprimido/tableta (tablet), cápsula (capsule), jarabe (syrup), gotas (drops), suspensión (suspension), gel/ungüento (topical).
